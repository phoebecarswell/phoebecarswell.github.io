about

Hi my name is Phoebe Carswell and I am an 18 year old student from New Zealand. I am currently studying a Bachelor of Design at Massey University in Wellington.

I have always had a passion for creativity and from a young age you could find me drawing or with paint all over my hands. My other passion is travelling and I have been fortunate enough to have seen a lot of the world at such a young age. I one day hope to pursue a career that allows me to do both of these things.

This website showcases my work from the past three years if you wish to work with me my contact information is on the contact page.
